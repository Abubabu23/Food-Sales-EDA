# Food Sales EDA

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on food sales data to understand **revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and peak sales periods**.  
The goal is to provide actionable insights that help improve **inventory planning, staffing, marketing strategies, and overall profitability**.



## Business Objective

To analyze food sales performance across **day, month, and year** in order to:

- Identify top-selling and least-selling products
- Understand revenue contribution by item and category
- Detect peak sales times
- Analyze transaction types and customer purchase behavior
- Support better business decision-making


## Key Analysis Areas

### Top-Selling Items & Categories
- **Highest-selling products:** Cold Coffee, Sugarcane Juice, Panipuri
- **Top-performing category:** Fast Food
- **Opportunity:** Introduce combo offers or discounts on beverages to increase sales



### Revenue Contribution
- **Top revenue contributors:** Sandwich, Frankie, Cold Coffee
- **Lowest revenue contributors:** Vadapav, Aalopuri  
  → Indicates potential areas for improvement or re-positioning



### Quantity Sold
- **Highest units sold:** Cold Coffee, Sugarcane Juice
- **Lowest units sold:** Vadapav, Aalopuri



### Transaction Type Behavior
- **Cash transactions dominate** over card payments
- Suggests opportunity to promote digital payments



### Peak Sales Time
- Highest sales observed during:
  - **Afternoon**
  - **Evening**
  - **Night**



### Time-Based Sales Analysis

#### Daily Sales
- **High-performing days:** 1, 10, 27, 30
- **Low-performing days:** 6, 18, 23, 28  
  → Targeted promotions can be introduced on low-performing days

#### Monthly Sales
- **May** recorded the highest sales

#### Yearly Sales
- **2022** outperformed **2023**

#### Weekday Analysis
- **Monday and Thursday** show the highest sales activity


## Key Insights

- Beverage items drive both **high quantity and high revenue**
- Certain products consistently underperform and need attention
- Sales are time-sensitive, with clear peak hours
- Customer payment behavior is heavily cash-based
- Data-driven strategies can significantly improve revenue and efficiency
